Hot & Spicy Chole

A popular vegetarian Indian dish made of chickpeas and spices. Serve with white rice or naan.

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
  • 1 small onion, diced
  • 1 teaspoon minced garlic
  • 1 teaspoon minced fresh ginger root
  • 2 star anise pods, broken into pieces
  • 1 (15 ounce) can chickpeas, drained and rinsed
  • 1 (8 ounce) can tomato sauce
  • 1 cup water
  • 1 teaspoon chile powder
  • 1 teaspoon garam masala
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon cumin & coriander

Steps

  1. Heat oil in a pot over medium-high heat. Saute onion until translucent, about 5 minutes.
  2. Add garlic, ginger, and anise; stir. Add chickpeas, tomato sauce, water, chile powder, garam masala, salt, cumin, coriander, turmeric, and clove.
  3. Reduce heat to medium-low and simmer, covered, until chickpeas start to soften, 5 to 7 minutes.
  4. Mash some of the chickpeas directly in the pot to give the sauce a chunkier consistency.
  5. Cover and continue simmering, stirring occasionally, until flavors come together, about 15 minutes.
  6. Remove star anise pieces and clove before serving.
